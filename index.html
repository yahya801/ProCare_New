About Us

Based in Germany, ProCare consolidates a fresh approach with more than six decades of of R&D,manufacturing, product innovation and brand marketing experience. With a vision to deliver high quality, value for money and honest products to the consumers, we aim to reshape your lives and those you care for. Our continuously evolving product range includes, hand wash, shower gel/body wash, hair color, hair removal cream, face wash, body lotion, sanitizer, insecticide, dishwashing detergent etc. Besides having our own range of brands such as Clearex, Aura Max Tox etc., we also have a dedicated Private Label division and manufacture private label products for renowned companies and retail groups.
